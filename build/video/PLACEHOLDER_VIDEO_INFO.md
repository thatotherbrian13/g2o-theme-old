## Video Specifications

**Required Format:**
- File Type: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) minimum
- File Size: Under 5MB (compressed for web)
- Duration: 10-30 seconds (will loop)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9

**Quality Guidelines:**
- Professional, subtle movement
- Not too distracting (it's a background)
- Dark overlay will be applied (#234253 at 70% opacity)
- Colors: Blue/teal tones preferred to match G2O brand

## How to Add Video

1. **Download** a suitable video from Pexels or Pixabay
2. **Compress** if needed (use HandBrake or similar)
3. **Upload** to: `/wp-content/uploads/changemakers/hero-video.mp4`
4. **In WordPress Admin:**
   - Edit the ChangeMakers 2025 page
   - In "Hero Background Video" field, upload your video
   - Adjust "Video Overlay Opacity" slider if needed
